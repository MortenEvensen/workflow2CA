About us

We are a Oslo based company founded in 2016. Our mission is to offer the ultimate tour of Oslo in an eco-friendly way. Many of the most interesting places in Oslo are not available by car or bus, but since Oslo is a big city a tour by foot would take to much time! Thats why we started Re-Cycle!

We offer the best tour available in an eco-friendly way. It's a win-win situation for everyone!
